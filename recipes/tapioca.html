Tapioca

Tapioca is a low calorie and gluten-free alternative to bread, and it is incredibly easy to make!

Ingredients

  • 50g of hidrated tapioca flour;
  • That's it, really!

Steps

  1. On a cold frying pan, pour the tapioca flour, making a thin layer;
  2. Turn the flame on high, and let it cook on one side for a minute;
  3. Flip the tapioca, and let it cook on the other side until done;
  4. Put on your desired fillings! Either sweet or savory goes really well with tapioca!
