Foccacia Bread

This bread is easy to make and delicious to eat. With just a few hands on times needed, this recipe is perfect for weeknight dinners or easy to make bread for a party!

Ingredients

  • 500g of all-purpose flour;
  • 300g of room-temperature water;
  • 10g of instant yeast;
  • 6g of fine sea salt;
  • 50g of extra virgin olive oil;

Steps

  1. In a bowl, add the flour, yeast and salt;
  2. Mix together until homogenous;
  3. Slowly mix together the water, until a wet dough is formed;
  4. If the dough is dry, add a little more water (10g at a time);
  5. While mixing, add the olive oil until homogenous;
  6. Once a wet dough is formed, and all olive oil was used, let the dough rest for 10 minutes;
  7. in intervals of 15 minutes, perform a series of stretch and folds, for a total of 4 times;
  8. After the last stretch and fold, pour the dough in a sheet pan with olive oil in the bottom;
  9. Add olive oil to the top of the dough, and let it rise, anywhere from 1 to 2 hours, or until doubled and bubbly;
  10. With the tip of your fingers, press the dough forming a wavy surface;
  11. Put it in a pre-heated oven at 230Â°C, for 30 to 40 minutes, or until golden brown in the top and cooked all the way through
  12. Take it out of the oven, and let it rest until cooled (if you can resist it)
  13. Enjoy ;)
