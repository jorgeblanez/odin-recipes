Fried Egg

Fried egg is easy to do, but hard to master. This is my wife's favorite when we don't know what to make for dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 (or more!) egg(s);
  • a teaspoon of olive oil or any other oil;
  • Salt and pepper to taste.

Steps

  1. Put a frying pan in the flame, and let it become hot;
  2. Once the pan is hot, add the oil;
  3. Break the egg on top of the hot oil, add salt, and let it sit for 10 seconds;
  4. Very careful not to break the yolk, flip the egg;
  5. Cook on the other side until the desired doneness;
  6. Plate it up!
